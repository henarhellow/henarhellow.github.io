A Brief Blurb About Me

I'm Henar Hellow and I'm an experienced researcher and data analyst. I have 6 years of experience helping different audiences engage with data to connect the dots in solving problems. I've spent a lot of time looking at data in commercial real estate, the labor market, and framing demographic data through a geospatial lens.

In another life, I got my B.A. in Studio Arts and a second degree in Asian Studies, emphasis in Chinese language. While I spent much of my time growing up involved in art and writing, on a study abroad trip to Singapore I was exposed to a whole new spectrum of disciplines and realized I wanted to pursue topics in international relations and policy. I was lucky enough to be admitted to the graduate school of Global Policy and Strategy at UCSD in my last year in undergrad, and dove in head first into environmental policy, econometrics, and a dizzying array of topics I had never known you could study and get a job in.

Now, in addition to my day job working on projects for clients in commercial real estate, I'm working on leveling up my R Shiny skills and geospatial skills using tools like leaflet and ArcPro. Returning back to my environmental roots in grad school, I'm also deep diving into ESG by taking the SASB FSA exam, analyzing environmental data, and learning how to use R's powerful interactive capabilities to create more engaging data visualizations that can tell the story in a compelling way.

Creating this website using github pages and jekyll has given me the excuse to learn github and html to put together a platform to showcase the R Shiny apps and interactive maps I work on.
